Mar 10 - Christmas? Packages came today! First a small box from Singapore containing $60 worth of displays. (not all for me...) Funnily enough the contents were marked as a gift on the customs forms, but they were honest with the dollar amount. Next, two much larger boxes arrived from DuPont containing the sample Pyralux kaptan circuit board material that I requested a couple of weeks ago. You have to give DuPont credit for being incredibly generous, I sent them an email asking if they would be willing to sell/send me a single sheet of one of their cheaper products since you can't buy the stuff in small amounts from distributors. After asking a few questions about my project they instead opted to send me 12sq. feet of two of their nicest materials. Obviously they don't have smaller-sized boxes, the few sheets of each material came in a large box with a few inches of cardboard and plastic padding. I've uploaded a couple of images of the displays and kaptan on the media page. (A few were taken with my phone camera and they're terribly fuzzy...)
